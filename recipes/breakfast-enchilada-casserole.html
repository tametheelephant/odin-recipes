Breakfast Enchilada Casserole

Home

Description

A delicious and hearty breakfast casserole with eggs, cheese, and enchilada sauce.

Ingredients

  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup cooked sausage or bacon (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, enchilada sauce, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the cheese, bell peppers, onions, and cooked sausage or bacon (if using) to the egg mixture.
  4. Pour the mixture into a greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the top is golden brown.
  6. Let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
