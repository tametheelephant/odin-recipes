Campfire Carnitas

Home

Description

Tender and flavorful carnitas cooked over a campfire for a smoky taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the pork, onion, garlic, orange juice, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the mixture in a heavy-duty aluminum foil pouch.
  3. Cook over the campfire for 2-3 hours, or until the pork is tender and easily shredded.
  4. Remove from the fire and let cool slightly before shredding the meat.
  5. Serve in tortillas with your favorite toppings.
